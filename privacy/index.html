Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

What We Collect

This site stores a small amount of data locally on your device to make your experience better:

  • Your display name — the name you enter when you first visit, used for the greeting on the homepage.
  • Starred games — which games you've favorited, so they appear in your starred section.

How It's Stored

Both pieces of data are saved using browser cookies and localStorage. This data never leaves your device — it is not sent to any server, database, or third party. There is no account system and no sign-up.

No Tracking or Analytics

This site does not use Google Analytics, tracking pixels, fingerprinting, or any form of behavioral tracking. There are no ads that track you. We don't collect IP addresses, device info, or browsing history.

Third-Party Content

Games hosted on this site may load external resources (e.g., the Ruffle Flash emulator from unpkg.com). These services have their own privacy policies. We do not control what data, if any, they collect.

Clearing Your Data

You can remove all stored data at any time by clearing your browser's cookies and site data for this domain. There is nothing to delete on our end because we don't store anything remotely.
